Driver2Driver




Alert Others

At Driver2Driver, we strive to make sure that drivers can communicate with other drivers on the road to alert them of issues, such as a dead tail light or an open trunk. Our ultimate goal is to provide drivers with a safer driving experience, and the ability and peace of mind that if you see a problem, you can help to correct it.

Mistakes Happen


We at Driver2Driver know that humans make mistakes. It's in our very nature to make errors, big or small. Unfortunately, at times, these errors can put others in danger, especially when it comes to automobiles. An open trunk or a broken blinker can lead to road hazards or misinterpretation. Most drivers are either not ware of the issue, or may not even know that what they are doing is putting others in danger. With Driver2Driver, you can easily message other drivers using their license plates to alert them of issues so they can be resolved.

Innovative Safety Features


Drivers need to keep their attention on the road, and that certainly takes precedent over infoming other drivers. As such we have enabled to following security features to assure drivers stay safe on the road:

  • Intelligent Voice Recognition
  • Manual input for Passengers only
  • Full Stop needed for Driver Manual input
  • Automatic License plate recognition (Tesla and Smart Vehicles only)
